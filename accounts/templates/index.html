    Products

    Scholar Byte

    One of the most sought-after solutions for school / college management. It is a feature-rich online product and a notch above the desktop version by virtue of its enhanced features and convenient mobile application that serves as an interface between teachers, parents and students.

    Scholars Desk

    State-of-the-art and value rich solution that has been patronized by a large number of schools and colleges. It caters to the core functions of a school / college. The product is popular with small and mid-sized educational entities.

    Scholars Eval

    The product helps conduct online examination – online, offline or a combination of the two. There is a provision for creating question bank and designing different types of formats for different exams / tests. The results can be published as per schedule. The application helps save time, paper and money besides offering accuracy.
